Welcome to Memento Amoris

This year, I decided to create Memento Amoris. It's a twist on the famous phrase 'Memento Mori,' which means 'remember to die'; 'Memento Amoris' is meant to remind us of our love and the journey we've shared together.
I have marked the places we have been to and curated pictures I think best represent those places. It's like a scrapbook, a virtual one, that I hope we can update and upgrade.

I hope you enjoy this as much as I do. Happy birthday, I love you.
